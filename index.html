LOSS STRATEGY
Focal Loss (γ=2) + Class Weights
Down-weights easy pixels. Flowers & Logs get 4× weight. Sky & Landscape get 0.4×. Forces attention on rare classes.
Dice Loss (0.4 weight)
Optimizes class overlap directly, independent of pixel frequency. Averages equally across all 11 classes.
FPN Multi-Scale Fusion
4 transformer stages fused top-down. Deep semantics guide shallow textures for sharper boundaries.
